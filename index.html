The Pardoned Prisoner

A prisoner will be pardoned if they can find a key hidden under a coin on a chessboard.

Player One (the Prison Warden) can:

  • Turn over any number of coins.
  • Hide the Exit token under any one of the coins.

  • Press the Offer button.

    At this point, the app will turn over a single coin.

  • Give Player Two control of this device.

Player Two (the Prisoner) can:

  • Tap on one of the coins.

If the Exit token is hidden under that coin, Player Two wins.
